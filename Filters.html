Activated Carbon: This filter works by absorbing the unhealthy chemicals. It chemically bonds to the water to remove the chemicals. This filter does not work on inorganic pollutants such as nitrate, and others. It is usually sold in the form of a block.


Distillation: One of the most classic, simple, yet effective methods to filter water. First, the dirt water is boiled and evaporates, creating clean vapour, which is then condensed back into clean water which can be immediately consumed. This method gets rid of anything that has a higher boiling point than water.


Solar Disinfection: This is a portable and inexpensive method which uses solar power to disinfect biologically contaminated water. (eg. viruses, bacteria, etc.)


P&G is a packet which is a powder that uses coagulants, flocculants, and chlorine to kill up to 99.999% of all the germs.
